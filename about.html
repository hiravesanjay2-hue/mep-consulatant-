About Devang Dalal Associates

Who We Are

Devang Dalal MEP Consulting Engineer is a premier multidisciplinary engineering consultancy firm headquartered in Mumbai. With a robust portfolio spanning HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Safety, and Integrated Building Management Systems (IBMS), our organization is committed to delivering innovative, safe, and sustainable engineering solutions.

We understand the importance of precision, energy efficiency, and cost-effectiveness, which allows us to provide tailored services that meet the unique requirements of each project. From concept design to execution and commissioning, we ensure the highest standards of quality and timely delivery across all our endeavors.

Our team comprises highly qualified and experienced engineers and technical experts dedicated to creating functional and future-ready infrastructure. We pride ourselves on our technical excellence, attention to detail, and our ability to adapt and innovate in the rapidly evolving landscape of modern construction.

Our Vision

At Devang Dalal MEP Consulting Engineer, our vision is centered around excellence, sustainability, and client satisfaction. We aim to be recognized as leaders in the field of MEP consultancy by consistently delivering solutions that combine modern technology, eco-friendly practices, and cost efficiency.

Our focus is on designing and implementing systems that not only meet today’s operational needs but also contribute positively to environmental conservation and long-term sustainability. Whether it involves high-rise commercial complexes, sprawling residential townships, data centers, or specialized institutional facilities, our goal is to enable our clients’ success through innovative and responsible engineering.

We aspire to partner with developers, architects, and project stakeholders to realize infrastructure that is safe, energy-efficient, and aligned with the latest standards in green building practices.

About Mr. Devang Dalal

Mr. Devang Dalal is the driving force behind the firm’s vision of engineering excellence. A seasoned Mechanical Engineer from Mumbai, Mr. Dalal brings over two decades of extensive industry experience. His professional journey began with prominent consulting firms such as Mr. Rumi H. Bharucha Consulting Engineers, where he served as a Senior Associate, gaining invaluable expertise in the design, planning, and execution of HVAC, Electrical, Plumbing, and Fire Fighting Systems.

Throughout his illustrious career, Mr. Dalal has demonstrated a strong commitment to sustainable development by leading numerous LEED-certified green building projects that have achieved Gold and Platinum Ratings from the US Green Building Council (USGBC). His dedication to environmentally responsible design allows clients to significantly reduce energy consumption and operational costs while maintaining optimal comfort and safety.

Mr. Dalal is an active member of prestigious professional organizations, including the Indian Society of Heating, Refrigerating & Air Conditioning Engineers (ISHRAE) and the American Society of Heating, Refrigerating, and Air-Conditioning Engineers (ASHRAE). His involvement in these associations keeps him at the forefront of industry advancements, ensuring that his firm adopts the latest technologies and best practices.

Recognizing the need for a holistic approach to building systems, Mr. Dalal founded Devang Dalal MEP Consulting Engineer — a firm driven by a passion for technical excellence, innovation, and sustainable development. Under his leadership, the organization has grown to serve a diverse and prestigious client base, providing end-to-end MEP consultancy services that encompass project planning, detailed design, execution, and maintenance support. His relentless pursuit of quality and client satisfaction continues to set new benchmarks in the industry.
