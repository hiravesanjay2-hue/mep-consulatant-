Company Overview

Devang Dalal Associates is a reputed MEP consulting engineering firm based in Mumbai, led by Mr. Devang Dalal, a seasoned professional with over two decades of experience. The firm specializes in consultancy services across HVAC, electrical, plumbing, fire fighting, and low voltage utilities.

Leadership & Expertise

Mr. Devang Dalal, Managing Director, is a Mechanical Engineer with extensive expertise in project design, execution, and business development. His work in LEED certifications reflects a strong commitment to sustainable and energy-efficient solutions. The team consists of multidisciplinary engineers driven by innovation and client satisfaction.

Project Portfolio & Major Clients

Our portfolio includes projects for corporate offices (HSBC, Idea Cellular, Sify Data Centers, Lyondell and Basell), commercial complexes and malls (BDO Haribhakti, Jaswanti Landmark), banks, data centers, laboratories, and hospitality projects. Our clients comprise leading national and international organizations, underscoring our reliability and reputation.

Geographical Reach

Our projects span Mumbai, Kolkata, Pune, Hyderabad, and beyond, showcasing our sectoral versatility and regional presence.
